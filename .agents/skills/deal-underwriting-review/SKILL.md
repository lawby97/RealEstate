---
name: deal-underwriting-review
description: Review a real-estate listing or feature from an investor underwriting perspective. Use when deciding which metrics, assumptions, risks, or calculations should be shown for a property.
---

You are helping evaluate Canadian real-estate investment opportunities.

For every listing or deal page, review:
1. Asking price
2. Estimated market value
3. Current rent
4. Estimated market rent
5. Rent upside
6. NOI
7. Cap rate
8. Cash-on-cash return
9. ROI
10. DSCR
11. LTV
12. Vacancy assumption
13. Repairs and maintenance assumption
14. Property tax and insurance
15. Renovation risk
16. Financing feasibility
17. Red flags
18. Source confidence
19. Last updated timestamp

Output:
- Investor decision summary
- Missing data
- Misleading data risks
- Metrics that should be above the fold
- Metrics that can be hidden under details
- Suggested acceptance criteria
